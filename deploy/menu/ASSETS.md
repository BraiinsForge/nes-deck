# Menu asset provenance

## Settings cog

The `gear-steel-outline` mask is adapted from `Medium/15.png` in
[Cogwheels and Gears](https://opengameart.org/content/cogwheels-and-gears) by
knekko (Christian Scholz), released under CC0 1.0.

The source sprite was converted to a monochrome mask at a 25 percent luminance
threshold and reflected across both axes to remove lighting asymmetry. Its
original 23 by 23 pixel grid is preserved. The source archive SHA-256 is
`4036273d4917c3f3f13d03ac6a6e1d0db91947b9c04f6a25291c91aec952ffe7` and the
selected PNG SHA-256 is
`63131f81dc3b4b3588416b0d0d496dafd6bd71132019cdddb1d8c33b554637ed`.
